About

Born in the shadows of the underground, DJ Raku is the architect of sonic landscapes that blur the lines between techno, house, and industrial grit. With a focus on deep, atmospheric storytelling and driving percussion, Raku has become a staple in the late-night club circuit.

Every set is a curated journey designed to lose you in the rhythm and find you in the sound. Welcome to the underground.
